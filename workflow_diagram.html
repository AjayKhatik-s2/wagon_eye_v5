WagonEye v4 · pipeline map

From four raw videos to one train inspection report.

A train rolls past four fixed cameras. This pipeline counts its wagons, inspects each one for open doors, damage, load state and its stencilled number, then ships a PDF + JSON report to S3 and email. Seven stages, one direction of flow.

INVARIANT

Only Stage 1 may count, number, or bound wagons. It produces the GlobalTrainState — the single source of truth. Every later stage reads it and adds detail; nothing ever recounts. RIGHT_UP is the master camera.

Pipeline · stage by stage

0
orchestrator/acquire batch

The conductor. Groups the 4 videos into one train batch and runs stages 1→6 in order. In --auto mode it also handles cameras that arrive at different times (resumable lifecycle).

4 × mp4→TrainBatch
1
reconstruction/ · wagon_count/count the wagons

Detects the gaps between wagons, tracks them, fuses the 4 cameras, and assigns GW_1…GW_N ids + ENGINE/WAGON/BRAKE_VAN class. Fails the whole batch if it finds zero wagons.

4 × mp4→global_train_state.json
2
materializer/cut into frames

Opens each video once and saves its frames as JPEGs, filed per wagon per camera. This is the last place video is ever decoded — everything after reads JPEGs from disk.

global_train_state+4 × mp4→wagon_cache/GW_n/<cam>/*.jpg
3
features/inspect each wagon · parallel

Four independent inspectors read the cached frames. Each is a thin driver; the real tracking/OCR intelligence lives in features/inference_lib/. One JSON answer + evidence snapshots per wagon.

door
open / closed / partial / damaged
door_state.pt
ocr
read 11-digit number
wagon_id_counting.pt + easyocr
load
loaded / empty
loaded.pt
damage
roof / floor damage
damage.pt

A failure is local: one bad wagon → NO_DATA cell, not a dead batch.

4
fusion/merge answers

Combines the four feature JSONs per wagon into one UnifiedWagonState using fixed authority rules, and raises the anomaly flags (door-open, damage, OCR-missing) that later highlight report rows.

door·ocr·load·damage json→unified/GW_n.json
4b
rendering/overlay videos · visual only

Re-draws boxes, ids and banners onto the raw videos for review. Runs no AI model — it only paints decisions already made and saved. One processed mp4 per camera.

unified + evidence→processed_videos/<cam>.mp4
5
reporting/build the reports

5a — four camera-wise PDFs, each scoped to what that camera owns. 5b — the combined train report (PDF + JSON, schema v4) with the KPI summary, per-wagon table, and damaged-wagon evidence grid.

unified + evidence→4 × camera.pdfcombined_train_report.pdf / .json
6
delivery/ship it · exactly once

Uploads the reports + evidence tree to S3 and sends one email per batch. A finalization marker guarantees a restart never uploads or emails twice.

reports→S3 archive1 email
